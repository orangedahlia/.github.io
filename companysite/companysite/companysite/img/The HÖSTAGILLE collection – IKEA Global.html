Tis the season for spook and spice!

The leaves are changing colour and there is the hint of chill in the air. And... could it be? Yes, that’s definitely the cosy aroma of a pumpkin spiced latte! Autumn is on its way and with it comes the HÖSTAGILLE collection. Take a look at two wonderfully wicked ways to bring some seasonal magic onto your table and into your home.

A cosy, colourful fall look

The candy house

Enter a world of sweet treats. But fear not! There is no wicked witch here - just companionable times and whimsical patterns. Simply by laying down a tablecloth, you've brought in a world of unexpected colour combinations. Pastels intertwine with rich, bold hues to create a magical forest of rosehip fruits.

HÖSTAGILLE tablecloth

A seasonal slice

Few things feel quite as cosy as a homemade pie bursting with sweet and tart fruits and berries. Treat your guests to a slice of something extra delicious, baked and served in HÖSTAGILLE style!

_

HÖSTAGILLE off-white pie dish and tea towels

Your “I’m never getting up” spot

It’s the season for snuggling in, so once the dishes have been magicked away, sink into your own little corner of comfort. Get lost in your favourite activity (be it reading or snoozing) and feel free to hibernate until dinnertime.

_

HÖSTAGILLE cushion cover

"I chose to paint the rosehip pattern for HÖSTAGILLE by hand in an autumnal palette with some contrasting colours to add more vibrancy. I hope it will spread a warm and cosy feeling in your home, year after year."

Designer Emma Prowse

Something moody this way comes

Double, double, toil and trouble... all newts, frogs and bats are welcome here. If you love the dark academia aesthetic, knock three times and a magical look will appear! 

_

HÖSTAGILLE artificial wreath

A midnight-themed feast

Were you one of those kids who waited patiently for their letter of acceptance to a certain wizarding school? An owl might not have arrived, but it doesn’t mean you can’t create your own magical banquet! HÖSTAGILLE wine glasses have a classic elegance with grooves that allow light to dance off their surface. Fill with a liquid potion of your choice and enjoy!

HÖSTAGILLE wine glass

Monochromatic bites

Are you on the hunt for a versatile look? Something that works for spooky themed parties and sophisticated autumn feasts alike? Try a minimalistic monotone aesthetic with elegant grey bowls filled with your favourite treats.

HÖSTAGILLE bowl

Little illuminations everywhere 

A warm glow and spicy notes of pumpkin and pies. Add candles of all shapes, sizes, themes and scents to your home for an immediate seasonal shift. From bat-themed pillar candles to tealight holders that project a forest of woodland beasts, the HÖSTAGILLE collection has it all!

HÖSTAGILLE tealight holder
